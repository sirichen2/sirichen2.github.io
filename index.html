About

Hi, I am a Master's student in Computer Engineering (Machine Intelligence & Applications) at National University of Singapore. Previously, I obtained my B.Eng. in Artificial Intelligence from Beijing Institute of Technology.

My research focuses on building general data agents to replace data scientists and engineers. I explore:

  • How to develop evaluation frameworks to measure the practical utility of data agents?
  • How to train cross-source heterogeneous data agents for optimal performance in real-world workflows?

I am the core creator of the first Chinese Mock Politeness benchmark. I am also a key contributor to widely recognized agent evaluation benchmarks, including DA-Code, DAComp, and DV-World. I also built a multi-center clinical dataset enabling cross-setting ML validation for pediatric myopia prognosis.
