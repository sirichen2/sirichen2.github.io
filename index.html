About

Hi, I am a Master's student in Computer Engineering (Machine Intelligence & Applications) at National University of Singapore. Previously, I obtained my B.Eng. in Artificial Intelligence from Beijing Institute of Technology.

My research focuses on building general data agents to replace data scientists and engineers. I am especially interested in how to evaluate practical utility in realistic workflows and how to train cross-source heterogeneous data agents for stronger real-world performance.

I am the core creator of the first Chinese Mock Politeness benchmark. I am also a key contributor to widely recognized agent evaluation benchmarks including DA-Code, DAComp, and DV-World. In parallel, I built a multi-center clinical dataset that enables cross-setting machine learning validation for pediatric myopia prognosis.
